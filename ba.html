Bachelor of Arts (BA) Program Information

Explore the world of arts with our BA program.

Program Overview

The Bachelor of Arts (BA) is a three-year undergraduate program designed to provide students with a strong foundation in arts and humanities.

Key Highlights:

  • Duration: 3 years
  • Degree: Bachelor of Arts
  • Curriculum: Comprehensive coverage of arts, humanities, and social sciences.

Admission Process

Admission to the BA program is based on merit and may include an entrance examination or interview. Eligibility criteria typically include completion of higher secondary education with a focus on arts or related subjects.

Curriculum

The BA curriculum is designed to provide students with a well-rounded education in arts and humanities. Core subjects may include:

  • Literature
  • History
  • Philosophy
  • Visual Arts
  • Performing Arts
  • Social Sciences

Career Opportunities

Graduates of the BA program can pursue various career paths, including:

  • Writer
  • Teacher
  • Historian
  • Artist
  • Researcher
